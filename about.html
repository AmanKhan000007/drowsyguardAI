About DrowsyGuard AI

Advanced AI-powered drowsiness detection for enhanced safety

Our Mission

To prevent accidents and save lives by providing real-time drowsiness detection technology that's accessible, accurate, and easy to use for everyone.

How It Works

Computer Vision

Uses MediaPipe Face Mesh to detect and track facial landmarks in real-time with high precision.

EAR Analysis

Calculates Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) to determine eye closure duration and detect drowsiness patterns.

Smart Alerts

Triggers immediate audio and visual alerts when drowsiness is detected to prevent accidents.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the detection?

Our system achieves 99.2% accuracy in controlled conditions. Accuracy may vary based on lighting, camera quality, and individual facial features.

Does it work in low light?

The system works best in good lighting conditions. Poor lighting may affect detection accuracy. We recommend adequate ambient lighting for optimal performance.

Can I use it with glasses?

Yes, the system works with most types of glasses. However, heavily tinted or reflective lenses may affect accuracy.

What browsers are supported?

Modern browsers with WebRTC support including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Camera permissions are required.
